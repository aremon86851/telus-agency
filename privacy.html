Privacy & Policy

Working toward finding you employment is our goal. Our main goal is to offer different kinds of jobs to eager workers who are looking for work but lack experience. There are certain limitations, though. In order to offer you a position, we need your information. because you must provide your details in order to be hired. Additionally, we will find you relevant jobs from other locations using your information.

We require your Data; why?

When you come to us with a possible employment, our main goal is to help you get the job you want. If you are an expert in any subject, please let us know what you have done and your educational background. We need adequate information from you to complete these tasks. When you provide us all of your information, we could use it to match you with the right job by comparing it to your information.

Where will we use the data?

We must give your information to that firm since we are providing you a job there. Additionally, I require all of your information so that I may provide it to the business and the individual who will handle your position. However, we wont divulge your information to anybody or anything that might damage you. So dont stress about it.

Safety:-

You have our unwavering assurance that we will protect all of your data. Additionally, we wont share your information with anybody without your consent. Only those who require information for the operation of your job will receive it from us.

So I will say in one word you dont have any tension about your data and its privacy we give you 100%
