Terms & Condition

Working toward finding you employment is our goal. Our main goal is to offer different kinds of jobs to eager workers who are looking for work but lack experience. There are certain limitations, though. In order to offer you a position, we need your information. because you must provide your details in order to be hired. Additionally, we will find you relevant jobs from other locations using your information.

Why do we require your data?

Our major objective when you approach us with a job potential is to assist you in landing the position you desire. We need enough information from you to do these jobs, and if you are an expert in any field, please let us know what you have accomplished and your educational background. When you provide us with all of your data, we may match you with the appropriate job by comparing it to your data.

Service Description

Our first responsibility is to find you work. You must first provide us with your personal information, after which we will compare it to job listings from other employers and assist you in finding the position you want.

Qualification

We anticipate hiring you with no qualifications. By offering you work, you will gain experience. Dont worry if you lack any skills or work experience; we will still be able to get you the job you desire. However, only if you adhere to our rules and behave appropriately in all of our interactions can we ensure that you will have a job.

Which Data do we need

Personal Information, Educational Information with CV, SSN Number, Driving license information, Email.
Your information will be given to other businesses so they can help you apply for the job you desire and get it. It’s 100% Safe for you. Don’t worry anything.

Having to Pay Money

No, we are not paying you for your work. We provide you with free assistance in finding your ideal career. For the past five years, Telus has worked mostly on finding jobs for the jobless. Furthermore, we have successfully placed over 3000 persons in the United States. Although we are not required to pay you anything, we will provide you with a job.
